Picture the number line: positives to the right of zero, negatives to the
left.

- **Adding** moves right: −3 + 5 = 2 (start at −3, take 5 steps right).
- **Subtracting** moves left: −3 − 4 = −7 (start at −3, take 4 steps left).
- **Subtracting a negative** flips to adding: 5 − (−2) = 5 + 2 = 7.

A sign flip turns a number into its mirror image across zero: the opposite of
3 is −3, and the opposite of −3 is 3.
