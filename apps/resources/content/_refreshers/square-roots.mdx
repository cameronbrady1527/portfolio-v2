The **square root** of a number is the value that, multiplied **by itself**,
gives that number. So √49 = 7, because 7 × 7 = 49.

A square root is **not** half the number: √100 = 10 (because 10 × 10 = 100),
not 50.

Roots of non-square numbers fall between whole numbers: √20 is between
√16 = 4 and √25 = 5, so it's a little under 4.5 (it's ≈ 4.47).
