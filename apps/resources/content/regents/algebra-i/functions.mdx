**Function notation** is just a label for "the output of this rule at this
input": $f(3)$ means substitute $3$ for $x$ and simplify — minding signs,
exponents, and piecewise conditions. **Transforming** a graph follows tidy
rules: shifting left by $k$ replaces $x$ with $x + k$, a constant outside shifts
up or down, and a factor in front stretches or compresses. These are **real,
released Regents questions**.
