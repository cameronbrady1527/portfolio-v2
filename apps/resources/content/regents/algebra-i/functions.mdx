**Function notation** is just a label for "the output of this rule at this
input": $f(3)$ means substitute $3$ for $x$ and simplify. **Transforming** a
graph follows tidy rules — shifting left by $k$ replaces $x$ with $x + k$. These
are **real, released Regents questions**.
