A **system** is two equations that must hold at the same time; its solution is
the point (or points) that satisfies both. These are **real, released Algebra I
Regents questions** — linear systems solved by substitution or elimination
(including word problems you translate into a system), and **linear-quadratic**
systems, where a line crosses a parabola in up to two places.

Every constructed-response item here is **solved algebraically** — set the
expressions equal, or substitute, and solve. Work each one on paper, then reveal
the official NYSED rubric and a worked model solution to score yourself; on these
multi-step problems, the credit is in the work.
