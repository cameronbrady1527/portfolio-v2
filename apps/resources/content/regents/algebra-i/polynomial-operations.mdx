Adding and subtracting polynomials is bookkeeping: **distribute** any coefficient
or subtraction sign, then **combine like terms** (same variable, same exponent).
The exponents themselves never change when you add or subtract — a classic trap.
These are **real, released Regents questions**; check each carefully.
