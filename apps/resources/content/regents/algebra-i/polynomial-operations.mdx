Polynomial arithmetic is careful bookkeeping. To **add or subtract**, distribute
any coefficient or subtraction sign and **combine like terms** (same variable,
same exponent) — the exponents themselves never change, a classic trap. To
**multiply**, distribute every term of one factor across the other (FOIL is just
this for two binomials), then combine like terms and write the answer in
**standard form**. These are **real, released Regents questions**; check each
carefully.
