---
title: CPCTC
subject: geometry
---

CPCTC stands for **Corresponding Parts of Congruent Triangles are Congruent**.
Once you know two triangles are congruent, every matching pair of sides and every
matching pair of angles is congruent too.

**How to use it.** Read the partners straight from the congruence statement, pairing
the letters in the same position: $\triangle ABC \cong \triangle DEF$ means
$A\leftrightarrow D$, $B\leftrightarrow E$, $C\leftrightarrow F$, so for example
$BC \cong EF$ and $\angle A \cong \angle D$.
