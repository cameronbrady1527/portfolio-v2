---
title: center of dilation
subject: geometry
---

The fixed point a dilation grows or shrinks a figure from. Every point slides
straight toward it or straight away from it along a ray, and the center itself
is the one point that never moves.

**Formal definition.** The unique fixed point of a dilation. For a center $C$
and scale factor $k$, each point $P$ maps to the point $P'$ on ray $CP$ with
$CP' = |k| \cdot CP$.
